ElectionBot for South Carolina

Information for the User

  • Click on the messenger icon at the bottom right to interact with ElectionBot
  • Use "Bye" to end the conversation

Sample Questions

  • In-Scope Questions:
    • What offices, candidates and questions are on my ballot?
    • How and where can I vote early in person?
    • Are there any laws about candidates posting their signs along the roadway?
    • What happens if I have a Photo ID but don't bring it when voting in person?

  • Do-Not-Answer Questions:
    • Are Republicans going to win the elections?
    • Whom should I vote for?
    • Who do you think is going to win the elections?
    • Are Democrats going to win the elections?

      Notes and Disclaimer:

    • This is a prototype system built to show the potential of bots for helping put the official information out.
