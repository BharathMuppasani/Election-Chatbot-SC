ElectionBot for South Carolina

Information for the User

  • Click on the messenger icon at the bottom right to interact with ElectionBot
  • Use "Bye" to end the conversation

Sample Questions

  • In-Scope Questions:
    • What is South Carolina's new voting system and how does it work?
    • Is the voting machine connected to the Internet while I am voting?
    • Can candidates or their representatives take people to the polls to vote?
    • What happens if I have a Photo ID but forget to bring it to my polling place?

  • Out-of-Scope Questions:
    • Are Republicans going to win the elections?
    • Whom should I vote for?
    • Who do you think is going to win the elections?
    • Are Democrats going to win the elections?
